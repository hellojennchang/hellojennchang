About

Sex is universal.

There's no shame in play.

We all exist because someone had sex. We're biologically wired for it. And yet we've built an entire world, our products, our language, our systems, designed to make us feel like our desires are wrong. So no, there's nothing wrong with you. That's a design problem. And design problems can be fixed.

I have spent over a decade in the wellness industry creating the products people reach for — whether it goes in or on the body. Making a good product is as much about understanding the person as it is about building the product.

My background is deliberately wide: BA² in criminal justice & sociology, certified holistic nutritionist, wellness product developer, UC Berkeley-trained graphic designer, AASECT-certified educator in training. I've worked with diverse populations across community health and elite biohacker communities.

I'm also obsessed with aerial arts because it embodies what I believe in - mindset, nutrition, sensuality, and somatic awareness.
